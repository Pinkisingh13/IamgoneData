Product Expiry Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

IamGone does NOT collect any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

IamGone ("the App") is a product expiry tracker designed to help you manage household items and reduce waste. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

IamGone does NOT collect any personal data. All data you enter (product names, expiry dates, categories, prices) is stored locally on your device only.

We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or phone number
  • Track your location
  • Send your data to any server
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or advertising SDKs

2. Local Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Hive, a local database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

3. Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you about expiring products. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

4. Camera & Barcode Scanner

The app requests camera permission only for the barcode scanner feature. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted.

5. Permissions Used

  • Camera — For barcode scanning (optional)
  • Notifications — For expiry reminders
  • Boot Completed — To reschedule notifications after device restart
  • Foreground Service — For background expiry checks

6. Children's Privacy

IamGone does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no advertising, in-app purchases, or social features.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated date.
